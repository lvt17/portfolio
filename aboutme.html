ABOUT ME

DORA-THUỲ DUNG

RMIT Graphic Design Sophomore | 2D & UI/UX.

I live by the mantra: 'Nothing is lost – it only changes material.' My mission is to bridge Vietnamese traditional culture with the digital revolution through interactive and modern design.

The Legacy Covenant

Nothing is lost, this is the core belief that the flow of Vietnamese culture is eternal. When silk, paper, or ceramics transform into pixels, vectors, and algorithms, their identity is not diminished; instead, it becomes more resilient within the digital timeline.

Standing at this intersection, I use technology as a tool to decode cultural depths, ushering traditional art into a new era, an era of interaction and perpetual presence.
