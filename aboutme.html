Who am I?

I see culture not as something static, but as a living system — shaped by memory, rituals, and the way we choose to tell stories today. Through art and design, I explore the intersection of tradition and technology.

Văn hoá còn thì dân tộc còn, văn hoá mất thì dân tộc mất.

When culture survives, the nation survives.

— Tổng Bí thư Nguyễn Phú Trọng, Hội nghị Văn hoá toàn quốc 2021
