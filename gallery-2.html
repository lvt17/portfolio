DRAGON OF
TRẦN DYNASTY

The dragon of the Trần Dynasty represents the peak of Vietnamese artistic expression during the 13th-14th centuries. Characterized by fluid lines, a more muscular body than earlier dynasties, and intricate tail details, this motif embodies strength and national resilience.
